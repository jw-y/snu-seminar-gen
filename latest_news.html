SNU Data Science Seminar “AI Custom Chip Enabling Sustainable AI Compute Infrastructure” Time : 09:00 - 10:30, Nov 12, 2025 Zoom: https://snu-ac-kr.zoom.us/j/2507466: Abstract The exponential growth of artificial intelligence (AI) has created an urgent need for sustainable compute infrastructure capable of balancing performance, efficiency, and scalability. This seminar introduces Rebellions, the only AI hardware unicorn in the Asia- Pacific region, and its mission to enable energy-efficient, open, and future-proof AI datacenter solutions. The talk highlights the paradigm shift in AI compute demand from training-centric to inference-centric workloads, where power efficiency and cost-effectiveness determine competitiveness. By fully integrating with open-source AI ecosystems such as PyTorch, Triton, and vLLM, Rebellions is democratizing access to high-performance inference hardware while breaking free from proprietary software dependencies. Finally, the presentation outlines how Rebellions’ co-optimized hardware-software stack, proven in large-scale commercial deployments with Tier-1 partners such as SK hynix, ARM, Samsung and Marvell, is shaping the next era of AI infrastructure—one that is sustainable, sovereign, and open-source-driven.
