SNU Data Science Seminar “Revolutionizing Semiconductor Innovation: The Power of AI/ML in Chip Design, Verification, and Manufacturing” Time : 09:00 - 10:30, May 7, 2025 Zoom: https://snu-ac-kr.zoom .us/j/2507466555 Abstract Modem semiconductor design and manufacturing are facing increasing complexity and efficiency demands, necessitating a shift from traditional methods. Over the last decade, machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI) have been progressively adopted in electronic design automation (EDA) to improve design Quality of Results (QoR) and to reduce tumaround times. Furthermore, AI and ML have been transforming diverse facets of the semiconductor sector, including chip verification, automated testing, and design-for- manufacturability strategies. This presentation will delve into how this trend, fueled by academic studies and the emergence of AI-driven commercial EDA tools, combines big data and cloud computing with ML, moving beyond conventional algorithmic techniques. Through practical illustrations of these advancements and future outlooks, this seminar will highlight how the strategic incorporation of AI/ML serves as a crucial driver for accelerating the semiconductor lifecycle, boosting efficiency, and enabling the development of next- generation microelectronic technologies.
