SNU Data Science Seminar “AT Revolution in Treasury Management - Data Science Applications & Startup Opportunities” Time : 09:00 - 10:30, Apr 16, 2025 Zoom: https://snu-ac-kr.zoom .us/j/2507466555 Abstract The rapid advancement of artificial intelligence is reshaping financial services, with treasury management emerging as a key area for disruption. This presentation explores how AI is transforming traditional treasury functions plagued by data silos, manual processes, and limited forecasting capabilities. With financial services AI investments projected to reach over $123B by 2032, applying machine learning, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning presents powerful opportunities for enhancing cash visibility, liquidity optimization, and risk management. The presentation outlines the structural and technical limitations of incumbent platforms—such as legacy architectures, siloed data, and slow innovation cycles—that hinder their ability to leverage AI effectively. In contrast, modem startups leverage API-first, microservices-based architectures, and domain-informed data science to deliver Al-native solutions tailored to treasury workflows.
