SNU Data Science Seminar “Design, Innovation, and Transformation in an AI World” Time : 09:00 - 10:30, Dec 3, 2025 Zoom: https://snu-ac-kr.zoom.us/j/2507466555 Abstract Innovation has always been the transformative force that drives organizations forward Often, technical breakthroughs unleash opportunities for organizations to leap ahead or fall behind. Al is yet the latest of these technology waves. We will examine the role of human-centered design in creating meaningful AI experiences, the importance of fostering innovation beyond technology adoption, and the cultural and structural shifts required for enterprises to thrive amid rapid change. Key themes include leveraging Al for creativity and decision-making, balancing automation with human judgment, and building resilient organizations that embrace continuous leaming. Attendees will gain insights into practical frameworks for integrating AI into products, services, and business models while maintaining ethical and responsible practices.
