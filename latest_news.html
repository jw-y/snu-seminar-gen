SNU Data Science Se “AI Systems Co-Design: Case Studies” Time : 09:00 - 10:30, May 14, 2025 Zoom: https://snu-ac-kr.zoom.us/j/2507466555 Abstract Recent advance in AI/ML is pushing the underlying systems to the limit in terms of scale and performance. AI systems co-design tries to address this problem by conducting an interdisciplinary research and development across AI/ML models, hardware, and software. Starting with examples of AI systems co-design, we discuss why it is important today. Using the large language model inference as an example, we will discuss interesting observations and recent developments around systems co-design including my anecdotal stories. Jason Jong Kyu Park Jason Jong Kyu Park is a software engineer at Meta, currently focusing on large language model inference. He previously worked at Google on Edge TPU. He received his Ph.D from University of Michigan based on research for GPU multitasking.
